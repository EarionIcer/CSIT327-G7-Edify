About Edify

Empowering Filipino Educators through Collaboration and Resource Sharing.

Project Overview

Filipino educators face challenges such as excessive administrative workload, limited access to relevant instructional materials, and lack of a centralized platform for professional development and collaboration.

Edify addresses these issues by:

  • Providing a centralized repository for lesson plans, activities, and multimedia resources.
  • Allowing educators to search, upload, and share content efficiently.

This project enhances teacher productivity, promotes continuous learning, and fosters a strong professional community, while offering hands-on experience in database management, web development, and user interface design.
